• Vivekanand Education Society’s Institute of Technology also known as VESIT or V.E.S. Institute of Technology, established in 1984, is an engineering college affiliated with the University of Mumbai
  • The campus is located in Chembur, a north-eastern suburb of Mumbai. VESIT shares its campus with the college of Pharmacy and the college for Management and Research. The campus comprises one big C-shaped building for the engineering college and several other buildings for the other colleges, hostels, etc.
